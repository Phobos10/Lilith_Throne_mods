Mod Name: Cancel orgasm (Other)
Mod Category: Status Effects, Items
Description: Scrolls the orgasm timer back.
It is relevant if the npc is limited by the orgasm limit and the player does not press the "cancel orgasm (denied)" button.
Use an item to get a status effect. Enter sex, hover your mouse over the status effect icon as much as necessary.
The npc status effect will break until its value is 0.
